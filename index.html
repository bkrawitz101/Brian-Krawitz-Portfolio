Bio

My practice is an exploration of light as a language. Starting in the underground electronic music scene, I began to see sound and light not as decoration, but as a medium for transformation.

I construct large-scale temples and light sculptures designed to be inhabited—containers for community coherence and personal resonance. Whether through physical steel and LED or digital metaverse sanctuaries, my work seeks to bridge the seen and the unseen.

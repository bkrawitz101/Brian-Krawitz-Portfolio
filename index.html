Bio

The Operator Behind the Signal

Brian Krawitz is a Creative Technologist and Light Sculptor operating at the convergence of digital systems and physical reality. With a focus on [en]lightening concepts, his work spans from intricate solo sculptures to massive collaborative temple structures.

Driven by a passion for regenerative futures and immersive storytelling, Brian weaves custom electronics, code, and structural design into experiences that challenge perception. From the playa of Burning Man to virtual landscapes in the metaverse, his portfolio represents a continuous exploration of how light and technology can elevate human consciousness.
